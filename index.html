John Doe

Indie/Folk Acoustic Guitar Player

John Doe is a passionate indie/folk guitarist based in the Pacific Northwest. With over 10 years of experience crafting heartfelt melodies and poetic lyrics, John's music captures the essence of nature, human connection, and the simple joys of life.

Influenced by artists like Bon Iver, Fleet Foxes, and Nick Drake, John's unique sound blends traditional folk with contemporary indie elements, creating an immersive experience that resonates with audiences of all ages.
